The Experience

Step into a realm where brutalist architecture meets transcendent soundscapes. Neon Horizon is not merely an event; it is an immersive auditory and visual journey curated for the discerning aficionado. We are transforming The Domain into a cathedral of electronic music, featuring state-of-the-art spatial audio and architectural lighting design that breathes with the rhythm.

Expect an uncompromising lineup of international selectors taking you through a progression of deep, hypnotic grooves into high-energy, peak-time techno. The dress code is strict: avant-garde dark wear. Prepare for an atmosphere of intense energy, profound connection, and unparalleled production quality.
